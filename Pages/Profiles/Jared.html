Jared Benson

Career Record 7-7

Jared "Jbens" Benson is best known as the league's serial trade rapist. Given his background as a collegiate athlete, some believe it may have been for the best if he had just stuck to basketball. Jared's 2022 campaign ended in shambles as he lost his last 5 weeks to finish at 7-7. After limping into the playoffs, he was decimated in the first round by Jesse Popper, who won 163.06-66.12.
